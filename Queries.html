Frequently Asked Questions

What is 3D anatomy viewing?
3D anatomy viewing allows users to explore human anatomy in a three-dimensional space, providing an immersive experience to learn about the body structures.
How can I navigate the 3D model?
Use your mouse to rotate the model, scroll to zoom in/out, and click to select specific parts for more detailed information.
Is the model interactive?
Yes! You can interact with the model by clicking on body parts to get more information about each part's function and structure.
